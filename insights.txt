Customer Churn Insights

1. Customers with longer tenure generate higher total charges, showing long-term value.

2. Monthly charges have a strong positive relationship with total charges.

3. Customers with higher monthly charges are more likely to churn.

4. Customers on month-to-month contracts show higher churn compared to long-term contracts.

5. Payment methods such as electronic check are associated with higher churn rates.

6. Senior citizens tend to churn slightly more than younger customers.

7. Paperless billing users show slightly higher churn behavior.

Conclusion:
Customer retention strategies should focus on high-paying customers, especially those with short-term contracts.
